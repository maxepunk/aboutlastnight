## DEEP BACKSTORY (the long fuse — from the Timeline DB)

- **2006** — Marcus, Sam (Derek), Mel (Diana), Nat (Sofia) meet at Stanford → "The Stanford Four."
- **2008–2010** — Sam + Marcus start throwing illegal warehouse parties; Mel books the first venue and talks campus police out of arrests (2007/2009 raves: Glow-in-the-Dark, Rodeo Night, Matrix Rave).
- **Jun 12, 2010** — Marcus, **Alex**, **Sarah** meet at the Facebook summer internship run by **Vic**.
- **2013** — Mel and Marcus have a brief affair.
- **Sep 12, 2014** — Sarah and Marcus marry.
- **2018 / Apr 2022** — Alex and Marcus co-found **BizAI**; take it to Y Combinator (2022). Marcus meets **Remi** at YC. Marcus sells BizAI (~$300M) and cuts Alex out — Alex engineered ~90% of the code.
- **2018** — Quinn (Oliver) abruptly kills his startup with Cass (Tori) and Zia (Leila) to **bury his PsychoTrophin research** → Quinn/Zia bad blood. (Origin of the leverage Marcus later uses.)
- **2020** — Ezra (Howie) runs an online neuroscience-tech incubator; his five mentees are Marcus, Cass, Quinn, Skyler, Zia.
- **2023** — Zia builds **The Oracle Ledger** blockchain algorithm; Marcus gives clandestine seed funding + BizAI data access (later steals it).
- **Jun 2024** — Vic's new VC firm funds **both** NeurAI (Marcus) and AIBioComp (Remi). Marcus starts paying Sam to manufacture **PsychoTrophin-3A** in a secret warehouse lab. Marcus founds NeurAI (incorporated Jul 8, 2024) on (allegedly) Alex's stolen idea/code.
- **2024** — Marcus commissions Cass's **Synesthesia Engine** ("Neuro-Aesthetic Protocol"); has her sign an NDA and buries it. Marcus hires Kai for party installations. Marcus's affair with **Jess** begins; she becomes pregnant (test collected May 2023 per the document; discovered/escalated late 2026). Ezra's EFF office is burgled (Oct 2024) — files from his 2020 program stolen.
- **Jan 2025** — Ashe writes the BizAI expose → **Taylor leaks it to Marcus** → editors bury it → **Ashe is fired** (Jan 31).
- **Late 2026 – Feb 2027 (run-up):** Marcus blackmails Quinn for more PT-3A (email Feb 17, 2027). Morgan arranges Marcus's congressional testimony on AI ethics. Riley uncovers Marcus's shell-company/environmental crimes (Blackwood Capital → Morgan Corp). Alex serves the **Cease & Desist** (Jan 28, 2027; deadline Feb 28). Remi emails Vic/Marcus about unfair funding. Sarah finds the paternity test and asks Marcus to leave; he drains their shared account. Morgan + Vic meet privately about "the Marcus problem." Sam's diary (Feb 17–20) tracks Marcus's decline and Sam mailing Sarah the paternity test.

Everyone arrives at the **NeurAI launch warehouse rave on Feb 21, 2027** already armed with a motive.

## THE FOUR-STAGE META-TIMELINE (the frame the article sits on)

The fiction above is only the first of four nested layers. The published article is written from inside this stack:

1. **The party (fiction, Feb 21–22, 2027, ~8 PM–~4:52 AM).** Everything in this document. Ends with Marcus's death.
2. **"The morning after" — the investigation (the REAL ~3-hour game session).** Players wake as the amnesiac partygoers, recover their **fragmented, locked memory tokens** (locked because of MAR004), scan them, trade evidence, and piece together the night. This is the actual escape-room/roleplay experience.
3. **The deliberation / vote.** The room collectively decides what happened and who is responsible — frequently turning against its own findings (the group authors the verdict; there is no hidden canon answer, so the "truth" is the gap between the record and the room's conclusion).
4. **Nova's same-day write-up.** The NPC journalist **Nova**, who crashed the investigation, writes the post-game **NovaNews investigative article** that evening — either **on-site** or **remote** (the reporting mode is a per-session config). Each session's article re-sequences this fixed fabula around whatever that room exposed, buried, and concluded.
