## What makes an ALN article compelling (generalized)
1. **Reads against the verdict** — finds what the accusation obscures, not a retelling of who was blamed.
2. **Built from this session's exact material** — could not be transplanted to another session.
3. **Honors what the ingredients permit/forbid** — account ownership asserted only where exposed; buried = amount/timing/behavior, never invented owners; buried contents = "the shape of silence."
4. **Throughline through every section, escalating, named only at the close**; first person calibrated to Nova's real access (present / floor-via-Ashe / fully remote).
