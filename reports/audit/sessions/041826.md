## 5. Interaction-model contribution

**Success pattern (reinforces the calibration thesis, adds a new wrinkle).** This is the strongest demonstration yet that the pipeline can read a "convenience-conviction + money-frame" session correctly *and* find a kicker beyond the obvious gap. Where 041726 stopped at "the punch was the pretext; the protected insider becomes CEO," 041826 fuses **three** ingredient signals the room never connected — (1) the chronology gap (Marcus "dead on paper" at 9:55 vs the 11:32 punch), (2) the director-flagged shell frames on Zia/Mell/Taylor, and (3) the off-page succession of **Sarah** — into a single thesis about **extraction-as-alibi-eraser**: "the people with the most reason to turn on a NeurAI founder are the people least able to account for themselves the morning after." The money-section evidence-boundary discipline is a model others should match.

**Failure signals (small, and instructive):**
1. **Roster-silence → pronoun invention** (Zia she/her) remains an unguarded seam: when a referenced-but-absent character has no roster pronoun, the pipeline defaults to a gendered guess instead of canon "they." Recurring across the corpus.
2. **Roster-vs-canon pronoun slip on a present player** (Mel "her/his"): even with the roster explicit (Mel = he), the strong canon/handle pull ("Mell," canon-default she) produced a mismatch. Suggests the refinement pass should diff every gendered pronoun against the roster, not just spot-check.
3. **Recipient-swap factual error** (Sam mailed the test to "Jess" in prose vs "Sarah" in the adjacent card): a single-sentence slip that contradicts the article's own evidence card — the kind of plausible-reading error this audit exists to catch.
4. **Stale `<title>`/`og:` headline** ("Broker" vs visible "Widow"): the headline-revision/meta-sync gap, now seen in 051126 *and* 041826 — a systematic refinement step (re-sync `<title>`/`og:*` to the final `<h1>`/deck) is warranted.
5. **Inference-vs-director-decode divergence** (Ladybug → Jamie, not Vic): not an error (correctly hedged), but a reminder that Nova's exposed-pattern reasoning can misroute a buried pile away from the very actor the thesis depends on; richer money-mapping would have strengthened the close.
