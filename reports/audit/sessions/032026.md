The supportable throughline, then: **the man is dead but the memory machine outlived him — and the night's actual business was deciding who gets to own the stolen consciousness.** Every thread feeds it: the IP thefts (Zia's Oracle Ledger, Cass's Synesthesia Engine, Alex's BizAI code) are the prologue establishing Marcus as a serial appropriator; the extraction protocol is the appropriation turned on human minds; the Black Market is that appropriation continuing *after* his death, now run by Blake (who collected everything and vanished).
