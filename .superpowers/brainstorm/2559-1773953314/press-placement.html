Where should press coverage live on the main page?

Three approaches — click to select your preference. These show placement strategy, not final styling.

A

"As Seen In" Press Bar + Upgraded Testimonials

Two-layer approach: A slim credibility bar near the top of the page (after the booking bar) showing "SF Chronicle • Tri-City Voice" — instant authority signal. Then the best press quotes replace 2 of the 4 anonymous testimonials, interspersed through the page with publication attribution.

Page flow:
Hero → Booking Bar → ★ Press Bar (NEW) → What Happened → ★ Press Quote (replaces testimonial) → Fragmented Memories → What Exactly Is This → ★ Press Quote (replaces testimonial) → Creators → Philosophy → Player Testimonial → FAQ → Player Testimonial → Booking Widget

Pros

  • Instant credibility at top of page
  • Press quotes distributed for sustained authority
  • Preserves some player testimonials for authenticity

Cons

  • Press bar is a new visual element to design
  • Two different treatments for similar content
B

Dedicated "In The Press" Section

Single section approach: A new "What They're Saying" section placed high on the page (after "What Exactly Is This"), featuring both press quotes with publication names, author attribution, and links to full articles. Anonymous player testimonials stay untouched.

Page flow:
Hero → Booking Bar → What Happened → Testimonial → Fragmented Memories → What Exactly Is This → ★ "IN THE PRESS" SECTION (NEW) → Creators → Philosophy → Testimonial → FAQ → Testimonial → Booking Widget

Pros

  • Concentrated social proof in one place
  • Doesn't change existing page rhythm
  • Easy to expand as you get more coverage

Cons

  • No press credibility until user scrolls deep
  • Visitors who bounce early miss it entirely
C

Hero Badge + Integrated Press Testimonials

Lightest touch: Add "SF Chronicle Datebook Pick" as a second line on the existing awards badge in the hero section. Then swap 2 anonymous testimonials for the press quotes (with publication attribution styling). No new sections or elements — just upgrades to what's already there.

Page flow:
Hero ★ (badge updated) → Booking Bar → What Happened → ★ Press Quote (replaces testimonial) → Fragmented Memories → What Exactly Is This → ★ Press Quote (replaces testimonial) → Creators → Philosophy → Player Testimonial → FAQ → Player Testimonial → Booking Widget

Pros

  • Minimal new code/design
  • "Datebook Pick" visible immediately in hero
  • Feels organic, not salesy

Cons

  • Press coverage less prominent than a dedicated section
  • Hero badge area could get crowded
