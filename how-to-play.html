CASE FILE: GAMEPLAY PROTOCOL

How to Play

Everything you need to know before entering the investigation

Meet Your Character

You'll receive a character sheet — your name, some memory fragments you still recall, maybe the name of someone you remember knowing. You don't know everything about who you are yet. Neither does anyone else.

This is your starting point. What you learn from here is up to you.

The Game

Unlock Memories — Marcus' 'lockdown protocol' has left evidence and extracted memories locked up all around the warehouse. Puzzle through how to get past the locks.

Scan Their Contents — Read the contents of the extracted memories using one of Marcus' memory display scanner prototypes.

Decide Their Fate — Memory tokens are your currency. Once you know the contents of one, you get to decide what to do:

EXPOSE

Turn the memory in as evidence. Everyone will see what happened.

Evidence types:

• Anonymous tip: Nobody needs to know you were the one to expose this memory

• Take credit: The public will know you were the source for this piece of evidence

BURY

Sell the memory for profit. The contents will be secret forever. $$ will be wired to your account.

Shell account:

• ANONYMOUS offshore account

• You CHOOSE the ACCOUNT NAME

• Shell accounts MAY be SHARED by MULTIPLE individuals

• LIMITED availability

Personal account:

• YOUR personal account

• Transactions CAN be TRACED BACK to you

• The CONTENTS of your sale remains SECRET

• May NOT be shared

TRADE

Leverage the memory to get something you need out of another player OR return it to its rightful owner.

Payment for each token varies based on its value to NeurAI. NeurAI prefers Technical > Business > Personal memories. Some memory-sets award 2-5x bonus payments for EACH MEMORY if sold as a set.

That's the loop. But you're not alone in it.

Others are unlocking memories too — about themselves, about you, about what really happened to Marcus. Not to mention, some puzzles require you to get information only others have access to.

What is found, what is shared, what is hidden... all of that is the game.

Negotiate — What's the story YOU want the world to find out About Last Night?

Want Justice? Profit? Secrecy? The choice is yours–but the group must get their story straight before the cops arrive.

Well, that and the incentives offered by NPCs: Expose your alibi to the public, embarrassing as it may be, and you're off the hook. Bury the right secrets, and you may become the next face of NeurAI...

The clock is always running.

The Morning After

You leave, hopefully before the cops bust your unofficial investigation and take you in for questioning... But the story doesn't end.

After the game, you'll receive a personalized Nova News report showing how your choices shaped the official story. You'll also catch narrative pieces you may have missed during your session.
